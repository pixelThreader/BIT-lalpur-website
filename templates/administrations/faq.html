Placement Activities:

The placement activities are carried out by a dedicated placement cell, comprising a team of student coordinators for training and placement under the supervision of faculty-in-charge. All possible guidance and support is offered to the students resulting in rewarding placement records over the years.

The Department of Computer Science at BIT Lalpur Centre is recognized as a premier centre for technical and professional education and students at the undergraduate as well as postgraduate levels have been consistently sought after by reputed organizations. The names include IT giants like TCS, CTS, Wipro, Infosys, Accenture, Satyam etc. with encouraging placement figures of upto 85% for even the BCA course.

The management stream has also experienced a remarkable increase in the level of placement for BBA as well MBA students with the last academic year realizing almost 90% of placement for the MBA students.
